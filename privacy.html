Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2, 2026

Stockview ("we", "our", or "us") operates a Shopify application that provides merchants with a unified inventory dashboard across Shopify and Amazon fulfilment channels. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Data We Collect

When you install Stockview, we collect and store the following data:

  • Shopify store domain — to identify your account.
  • Shopify OAuth access token — to read inventory and product data from your store via the Shopify Admin API.
  • Shopify inventory data — product names, SKUs, variant images, inventory quantities per location. We do not collect customer names, addresses, orders, or payment information.
  • Amazon SP-API credentials — refresh token and seller ID, provided voluntarily when you connect an Amazon account. Used to read FBA and AWD inventory levels.
  • Amazon inventory data — FBA inventory quantities, days-of-supply metrics, and AWD stock levels per marketplace. No customer order or personal data is accessed.
  • App settings — your preferences such as which marketplaces are enabled, location visibility settings, and stock alert thresholds.

We do not collect or store:

  • Customer personal data (names, emails, addresses, payment details)
  • Order history or transaction data
  • Any data beyond what is necessary to display the inventory dashboard

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To display real-time and cached inventory levels in the Stockview dashboard.
  • To sync inventory periodically from Shopify and Amazon on your behalf.
  • To store your app configuration and preferences.
  • To respond to mandatory GDPR and Shopify data requests or deletion requests.

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Data Storage and Security

All data is stored in a managed PostgreSQL database (Neon) hosted in the European Union. Access tokens and credentials are stored encrypted at rest. We use HTTPS for all data in transit. Access to production data is restricted to authorised personnel only.

4. Data Retention

  • Active merchants: Data is retained for as long as the Stockview app is installed on your store.
  • After uninstall: We retain data for up to 48 hours to allow for reinstallation. After 48 hours, all store data is permanently deleted in response to Shopify's shop/redact webhook.
  • On request: You may request immediate deletion of your data at any time by contacting us at hello@getstockview.com.

5. Third-Party Services

Stockview integrates with the following third-party services:

  • Shopify Admin API — to read inventory and product data. Governed by Shopify's Privacy Policy.
  • Amazon Selling Partner API (SP-API) — to read FBA and AWD inventory. Governed by Amazon's Privacy Policy.
  • Vercel — application hosting provider. Governed by Vercel's Privacy Policy.
  • Neon — managed PostgreSQL database. Governed by Neon's Privacy Policy.

6. Your Rights (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area, you have the following rights:

  • Right of access — you may request a copy of the data we hold about your store.
  • Right to erasure — you may request deletion of all data associated with your store at any time.
  • Right to restriction — you may request that we limit how we process your data.
  • Right to portability — you may request your data in a machine-readable format.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at hello@getstockview.com.

7. Shopify GDPR Webhooks

Stockview responds to all three mandatory Shopify GDPR webhooks:

  • customers/data_request — we do not store any personal customer data, so no export is required.
  • customers/redact — we do not store personal customer data. No action required.
  • shop/redact — all data associated with the shop (credentials, inventory cache, settings, sessions) is permanently deleted.

8. Cookies

Stockview does not use cookies for tracking or advertising. Shopify may set session cookies as part of the OAuth authentication flow. These are strictly necessary for the app to function within the Shopify admin.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
